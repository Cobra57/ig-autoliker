Login to IG-Liker.

Account Privacy Settings:Before utilizing this tool, be sure that your account is not set to "Private" mode. Otherwise, the auto-liker will not function properly, and you will end up with a lot of follow requests.
First time login:When you use IG-Liker for the first time, you may receive a notice that says something along the lines of "Your account was logged into recently. Was this you?" Just click "This was me" on the popup window.
Account Safety:You will use your Instagram username and password to log in to IG-Liker for the first time. The only people who will see your credentials are you and Instagram. The information does not move through our servers in any way. Otherwise, this web page wouldn't be up. Instagram sends a signal to our servers that your username and password are correct, and then our server will generate cookies once. We pledge we will NEVER misuse your login information on your behalf. We do not even have access to it. You are absolutely safe while using this tool.
READ THIS: Sometimes, the service will fail, and you will be redirected to an unreachable webpage. This could be because you entered your username and password wrong, or it could be our fault. In the event that the problem is on our end, and the error keeps occuring, we will award you 50 followers for your inconvenience. You're welcome, by the way.
